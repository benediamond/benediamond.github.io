Benjamin E. DIAMOND

I'm a

I earned my PhD in math in 2017 from Johns Hopkins, where I studied algebraic geometry. My advisor was Caterina Consani. Since then, I've worked as a cryptographer at J.P. Morgan, Coinbase, and Irreducible. I specialize in algebraic geometry and zero-knowledge proofs.
